Wheat Cultivation Facts. Widely known as the most important of all the food crops, Wheat is the most grown crop in India. It is being grown in India for more than 5000 years & the new age wheat i.e. Triticum aestivum or the common Bread Wheat is being consumed all over India.

Sirisena Gamage Dryland farming

Dryland farming and dry farming encompass specific agricultural techniques for the non-irrigated cultivation of crops. Dryland farming is associated with drylands, areas characterized by a cool wet season that is followed by a warm dry season.Additionally,arid-zone agriculture is being developed for this purpose.
